About this text case converter

CaseForge is a free online tool for converting text between uppercase, lowercase, title case, sentence case, and playful formats like alternating and inverse case. Writers, developers, and students use case converters to quickly reformat headlines, code snippets, social captions, or pasted content without retyping a single word. Alongside conversion, this tool runs a live analytics dashboard that tracks word count, character count, sentence count, and estimated reading time as you type, making it useful for meeting content limits on platforms like Twitter, meta descriptions, or academic word counts. Everything runs directly in your browser: no text is uploaded or stored on any server, so your content stays private. Simply paste your text, pick a case style, and copy the result instantly.
